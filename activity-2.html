How to cook indomie

  1. Put Indomie Special Chicken Flavor in boiling water for 3 minutes without adding the seasoning. Lift, drain. 🍲🫕
  2. Boil eggs.🥚
  3. In another pan, heat the cooking oil 🍳
  4. Pour the stir-fried onion with fresh milk, add the cayenne. 🧅
  5. Turn off the heat. 🔥
  6. Pour into a serving bowl. 🍜
  7. Ready to serve. 😊
